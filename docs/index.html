🏥 Medsuite-eT

Complete Pharmacy Management System for Bangladesh

MySQL-backed SaaS with PWA support, role-based access, and comprehensive inventory management

🚀 Launch App ⭐ View on GitHub
ℹ️ Demo Site: This is the GitHub Pages landing page for Medsuite-eT. The app runs locally at http://localhost:8080 or on your deployed server.
💊

Inventory Management

Track medicines, batch numbers, expiry dates, stock levels, and minimum thresholds with real-time updates.

🛒

POS System

Quick and efficient point-of-sale interface for fast transactions and customer billing.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Sales reports, revenue tracking, low stock alerts, and expiry date notifications.

👥

Role-Based Access

Super Admin, Admin, Staff, and Customer roles with granular permission management.

📱

Mobile Ready

Progressive Web App (PWA) support for offline access and mobile-friendly interface.

🔐

Secure Backend

MySQL database with JWT authentication, encrypted passwords, and secure API endpoints.

📦

QR & Barcode

Generate and scan product barcodes for quick inventory tracking and verification.

🌐

Multi-Language

Full English and Bengali language support for interface and product information.
